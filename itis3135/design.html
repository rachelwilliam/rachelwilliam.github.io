Project Design and Proposal

Project Name: Deb's Dogs

Project overview:

  • Application and Purpose:
    This website will be used to help my client find good homes for her three dogs.
  • Intended Users:
    People who want to open up their homes to a dog.
  • Overview of Content:
    The website will give information about the different breeds, have pictures of each dog, and give some information about them, and finally have an interest form.
