Basic Needs

Food

Since Charlie, Teddy, and Echo are small dogs, they do not eat a lot. It is important to give them tasty kibble that provides all the nutrients that they need. Their favorite treats are Dentastix, Milk Bones, and Cheerios.

Charlie: 3/4 cup of Blue Buffalo dog food, twice a day (morning and evening)

Teddy: 1/4 cup of Blue Buffalo dog food, twice a day (morning and evening)

Echo: 1/4 cup of Blue Buffalo dog food, twice a day (morning and evening)

Exercise

Exercise plays a big part in keeping dogs healthy. Dog owners tend to be more active, so this is a win-win!

Charlie: 10 minute run in the morning, 15 minute walk or 10 minute run in the evening, and 10 minutes of fetch outside

Teddy: 15 minute walk in the morning, 15 minute walk in the evening, and fetch

Echo: 10 minute walk in the morning, 10 minute walk in the evening, and fetch till he wants to go back inside

Grooming

Charlie, Teddy, and Echo are hypoallergenic breeds, which means that they do not shed for the most part! However, they are also considered to be high-maintenance breeds. Deb currently takes them to the PetSmart closest to her house to get them groomed.

Charlie: groom once every 6-8 weeks, bathe once every 3 weeks, and brush every day

Teddy: groom once a month, bathe once every 2-3 weeks, and brush every 2-3 days

Echo: groom once every 6-8 weeks, bathe once every 3 weeks, and brush every day
